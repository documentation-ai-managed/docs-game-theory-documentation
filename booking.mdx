## Booking Process

Booking facilities on Game Theory is simple and straightforward. Our platform allows you to reserve courts, pools, and equipment in advance, ensuring you always have access when you need it.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Access your Game Theory account through the mobile app or website.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Facility" icon="search">
    Choose from available sports facilities like badminton courts or swimming pools.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Time Slot" icon="clock">
    Pick your preferred date and time slot from the available options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Booking" icon="check-circle">
    Review details and complete the reservation with instant confirmation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Bookings can be made up to 7 days in advance, with a maximum duration of 2 hours per session.
</Callout>
