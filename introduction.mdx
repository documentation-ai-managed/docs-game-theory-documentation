## Getting Started

Game Theory is a comprehensive sports membership platform that provides access to premium facilities for swimming, badminton, basketball, squash, pickleball, padel, football, and table tennis. With an all-access membership starting at ₹2,746 per month, members can join games, learn new sports, and utilize gym facilities all under one subscription. This platform empowers sports enthusiasts to stay active and connected through seamless booking and community engagement.
