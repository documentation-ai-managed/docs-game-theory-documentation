## Membership Overview

Game Theory offers flexible membership plans designed to fit different lifestyles and activity levels. Our all-access membership provides unlimited access to all sports facilities and activities, making it easy to explore new sports or stick to your favorites.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose Your Plan" icon="credit-card">
    Select from our membership tiers starting at ₹2,746 per month for full access to all facilities.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign Up Online" icon="user-plus">
    Complete the registration process through our secure online portal.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Playing" icon="play">
    Begin booking facilities and joining games immediately after activation.
  </Step>
</Steps>
